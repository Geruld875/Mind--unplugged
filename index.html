📘 Introduction

Mind Unplugged is a light but effective 7-day digital detox challenge. It’s for students who want to break free from distractions like betting, social media, or screen addiction, especially during high-focus periods like exam preparation. It’s not about quitting your phone — it’s about learning to use it better.

📌 FAQ

1. Why is this digital detox online?

Because we’re learning to use our phones more intentionally — not abandoning them. The program teaches you to control how you use your phone instead of letting it control you.

2. Do I have to stop studying on my phone?

No. This detox helps reduce unhealthy phone use while supporting academic productivity.

3. What do I have to do in this challenge?

Each day you get a task and a short reminder. You track your progress using a digital passport. You can also reflect privately and submit anonymous confessions as part of your healing.

4. What do I gain from joining?

More focus, better self-control, improved sleep, reduced anxiety, and a certificate of completion if you complete the 7 days.

5. Will anyone monitor me?

No. This is a self-led journey. You choose how deeply you engage. Your confessions are anonymous and safe.
